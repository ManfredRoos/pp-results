What am I seeing Here? Below, you see the results of Metastudent, a method to predict Gene Ontology (GO) terms via homology to already annotated proteins.
Select one of the two functional categories (Molecular Function Ontology or Biological Process Ontology) to display both a tabular and a graphical view. In the table, the GO terms are ordered by reliability (0-100) - the higher the reliability, the higher the chance that the prediction is correct. The graph additionally shows where the predicted GO terms lie with respect to their parent terms. The predicted terms are shown in yellow boxes, inferred terms in white boxes. Inferred terms describe functions not as precisely as predicted terms, but should be considered more reliable.
